Welcome to Idxport

Indonesia has an abundance of raw materials as well as exceptional craftsmanship and manufacturing standards that are rapidly evolving. Local production, on the other hand, is frequently unable to meet the customer service requirements and quality control standards necessary to compete in global trade.

Idxport provides tailored consultancy services to assist you in locating and dealing with the best manufacturers, negotiating with local suppliers, conducting quality control, and coordinating shipments from the factory to your destination port.

Our main focus is on delivering efficiency, ensuring the highest quality at the lowest possible price in the shortest timeframe possible for you and your clients, while supervising and inspecting every step of the way. We believe that this is the key to our collaboration's longevity and success.


Our core values are founded on the following principles:

  • Accountability in procurement, manufacturing, and shipping
  • Delivering the best performance with the utmost integrity
  • Assuring that our clients' specific needs and goals are met
  • Providing outstanding client support and management service
  • Attention to details & transparency - communicate whenever in doubt
  • Our commitment to quality control begins before production

Joint Ventures

Supply chain financing can be a powerful tool and a lever for scaling your business. Idxport can help our importers with a sufficient trade history to raise capital when the production demand increases but the manufacturers are unable to provide consignment or CIF payment terms to support the scaling process.

We can also become your partner in joint ventures and assist with vertical, backward, or forward integration where our assets and capital can be utilized to play an invested and more supporting role in production or help to further streamline the merchandising and distribution process.

Proficiency services

For a variety of items, our quality control third party services offer the toughest quality control in Indonesia. Your products will receive a customized version of our inspection and testing service.
To ensure a safe and successful production process for you, we conduct an unbiased evaluation of the manufacturer's profile. Our main priority is to ensure the process is safe and of high quality.
