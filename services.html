Idxport Services

In an ever-changing competitive environment, businesses must always be ready to cope with changes and be able to find a reliable intermediary to negotiate deals with local suppliers. Idxport understands what’s at stake. It is not just products that we are sourcing; the end user's expectation of quality is what will determine the future commitment and demand for the products' brand and supplier. It is of paramount importance that we achieve the best quality at the most reasonable pricing options through ongoing local negotiations.

Our team members possess in-depth market supply knowledge to identify and source products, handle professional negotiations with the local supplier, keep quality high but costs low, and conduct quality audits.

We value the consistency in our client’s relationship with the suppliers and ensure that every interaction is fully understood, and verified for all parties to prevent any misunderstanding.

 

Product Sourcing

We begin by identifying the sourcing specifications and requirements before evaluating the potential suppliers' choices. Once our research has started, we will provide you with access to our detailed analysis and profile of supplier options. The process can include a business assessment check, factory email audit, field audit, supplier qualification evaluation report, and manufacturing capability evaluation.

We will provide accurate, real-time insights revolving around identifying and assessing potential markets and suppliers. Our process will help you pre-qualify the suppliers based on quality, timely delivery, competitive pricing, and any other requirements that you may require.

 

Intergrations & Partnerships

Sourcing partnerships are a collaborative approach in sourcing where a buying organization or a small number of its suppliers work closely together, sharing the risks and rewards of a cooperative relationship that focuses on continuous improvement. Idxport is well placed to help you find the best verticle backwards integration partner and provide with the support for any upstream and downstream activities throughout the intergration process.

Idxport can also participate in joint ventures where assets can be used to participate as an invested partner and play an active role in the merchendizing and distribution processes.

 

 

Quality Control

At Idxport, we assist our customers in tailoring the inspection criteria for specific requirements. Inspection guidelines include general inspection criteria, sample feedback, and lab testing. At close proximity, we are able to improve the timeframe and cost savings from the manufacturing qualification process.

With ongoing procurement, we are able to keep your manufacturers at bay with our random inspection service for quality control, verification of product authenticity, microbiology, organic compound testing (material consistency), and or any test of your choosing during the manufacturing process or before loading.

This service is available for products sourced by Idxport as well as for products by any existing suppliers.
