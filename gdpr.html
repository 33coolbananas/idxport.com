1. Definitions of terms for the General Data Protection Regulation

“Personal Data” means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
“Laws or ordinances” means the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and laws of related countries.
“Processing” means any operation or set of operations which is performed on personal data or on sets of personal data, whether or not by automated means, such as collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation or alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination or otherwise making available, alignment or combination, restriction, erasure or destruction.

2. Applicable Scope of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is effective only if you locate in the European Economic Area (EEA).
If you are outside of the EEA, Different Privacy Policy will apply to you.

3. Processing of Personal Data

Idxport will process personal data only to the extent necessary to achieve the objectives stipulated in this Privacy Policy, and execute processing lawfully and rightly. In addition, if you are under 16 years old, we will process your personal data with the consent of the holders of parental responsibility over you.

Personal Data that we receive from you.

The above data may include information such as your name,address, phone number, birthday, e-mail address, amateur radio call sign, login ID and password, payment information such as bank information, driver's license, government issued identification number such as passport (in case such is required by laws or ordinances), pictures and signatures, information on employment

Personal Data generated when you receive our services.

The above data may include information such as your buying history of our products, services usage history, cookies and similar identifiers created at the point of use such as our website and/or the IP address of the electronic device used for accessing our website. With regards to cookies, please check the Cookie Policy.

Idxport will not process any sensitive personal data such as racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, health information, except as stipulated by laws or ordinances.

In the provision of customer satisfaction, Idxport may inform you of information related to our products and services by e-mail or mailing etc. If you wish to suspend the receipt of e-mails from Idxport, we will take measures to stop delivery promptly after notification of such a request.
Idxport uses “Google Analytics” to understand the usage situation of the homepage on this site. Idxport also use “cookies” provided by Google Analytics, but we do not collect any information to identify individuals through Google Analytics.

The data collected through your use of Google Analytics is managed based on “Google's Privacy Policy”. Please refer to the Google Analytics website for Google Analytics terms of use and privacy policy.

4. The Purposes of Processing

We process personal data for the following purposes:

  • Member registration of our services, confirmation of the identity necessary for the provision of services;
  • Provision of information about our products and services by e-mail or mailing, questionnaire survey;
  • Grasp and analyze status of use by product buying history, browsing history of our website, etc.;
  • Affairs such as application at administrative organization;
  • Security retention, communication and report in case of security issues;
  • Audio and video recording for crime prevention;
  • Answering to your inquiry;
  • Recruiting activity;
  • Answering to inquiry of administrative organization.

5. The Legal Basis for Processing

  •  Idxport may process personal data in the following cases:
  • Based on your consent;
  • To achieve the objectives stipulated in this Privacy Policy;
  • For entering into, or performance of, a contract;
  • For compliance with our legal obligation;
  • When processing is necessary to pursue our legitimate interests;
  • When Idxport determines that it is necessary to pursue legitimate interests of a third person/party who are required to disclose personal data.

6. Your Rights

 You have the right to receive an explanation and request disclosure about your personal data processed by Idxport. In addition, you have the right to request correction for incomplete or incorrect data, and delete your own personal data possessed by us.
You have the right to raise objections about data processing by us. Idxport is subjected to the limitation of processing extent if you raise any objections, we will be able to continuously process personal data only to the extent permitted by laws or ordinances.
You have the right to withdraw consent about the personal data processing any time if the processing is based on your consent.
If you enforce the rights set forth above, Idxport will respond promptly and in accordance with laws or ordinances and our internal regulations only in cases in which we can confirm that you are the identical person or agent having a legitimate right.
In addition to the above rights you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority about our personal data processing.

7. Proper Management

The period of privacy data to be kept by us shall not exceed the period necessary to achieve the purpose of processing as stated in this Privacy Policy. In addition, Idxport conducts strict security measures to prevent unauthorized access, tampering, destruction, leaks, loss, etc. by implementing appropriate security measures for personal data processing.

For access to databases etc. related to personal data, Idxport strictly limits access to only those employees who have access rights and strictly manage all access to information so that it will not be illegally used, even within the company.
Personal data can only be used by Idxport, and when disclosing statistical results processed into a form that cannot be specified by an individual, except in cases where it is based on laws or ordinances permit. Idxport will never transfer such information to a third person/party excluding subsidiaries, unless express permission is obtained.

8. Compliance with Laws and Other Norms

Idxport complies with the laws or ordinances concerning personal data, the guidelines established by administrative organizations, trade associations, etc., and we strictly observe the matters specified in our "Compliance Regulations (internal regulations of our company)".

9. Education · Communication

Idxport will conduct education related to personal data protection, in-house public relations activities, etc. to make employees aware of and improve their awareness of such.

10. Continuous Improvement

Idxport will respond appropriately to changes in the circumstances surrounding personal data and regularly review the matters specified in our internal regulations and make all reasonable efforts to continuously improve.
